How to Sign In to Your Ccleaner Account

Sign In

Ccleaner is a globally trusted PC cleaning and performance optimization tool. While the software works great in its free version, users who purchase the Professional edition or subscribe to multi-device licenses will benefit from having a Ccleaner account. Through your Ccleaner account, you can manage licenses, download software, track billing, and receive support—all from one dashboard. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to sign in to your Ccleaner account and make the most of your subscription.

Why You Need a Ccleaner Account

Creating and signing in to your Ccleaner account provides multiple advantages:

  • Manage your Ccleaner licenses across devices
  • Renew or cancel subscriptions
  • Download the latest version of the software
  • Submit and manage support tickets
  • Access billing history and download receipts

How to Sign In to Your Ccleaner Account

To access your Ccleaner account:

  1. Go to Official Website
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Enter your account password
  4. Click the “Sign In” button to access your dashboard

If you don’t have an account, click on “Create an Account” on the login page. Make sure you use the same email you used during your purchase.

Features Available After Signing In

Once signed in, you will be able to:

  • Activate your license on new devices
  • Download full software installers
  • Review all your subscription statuses
  • Update your billing preferences
  • Contact Ccleaner support via ticket or live chat

Resetting Your Password

If you’ve forgotten your password:

  • Go to the login page
  • Click on “Forgot Password?”
  • Follow the instructions to reset your password via email

Troubleshooting Sign-In Problems

If you’re unable to log in, try the following:

  • Double-check email and password spelling
  • Try a different browser or clear your cache
  • Make sure you're on the official site:
  • Still stuck? Use the Zendesk widget below to chat with support

Keeping Your Account Secure

To secure your account and personal information:

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Change your password every few months
  • Never share your login credentials
  • Log out after accessing your account on shared computers

Conclusion

Signing in to your Ccleaner account gives you full access to features like license control, technical support, and software management. It’s the best way to stay in control of your product and ensure it works smoothly across all your devices. If you haven’t signed in recently, visit Ccleaner Account Sign In and get started today. For any help, use the Zendesk support widget at the bottom right of your screen.
